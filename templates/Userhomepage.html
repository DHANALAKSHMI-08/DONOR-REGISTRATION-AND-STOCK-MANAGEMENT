Welcome to Blood Bank

Here are some important facts about blood groups:

A+

  • Can donate to: A+, O+
  • Can receive from: A+, A-, O+, O-

A-

  • Can donate to: A+, A-, AB+, AB-
  • Can receive from: A-, O-

B+

  • Can donate to: B+, AB+
  • Can receive from: B+, B-, O+, O-

B-

  • Can donate to: B+, B-, AB+, AB-
  • Can receive from: B-, O-

AB+

  • Can donate to: AB+
  • Can receive from: Everyone
  • Universal Receiver

AB-

  • Can donate to: AB+, AB-
  • Can receive from: A-, B-, AB-, O-

O+

  • Can donate to: A+, B+, AB+, O+
  • Can receive from: O+, O-

O-

  • Can donate to: Everyone
  • Can receive from: O-
  • Universal Donor
